About Monkeys

Monkeys are a diverse group of primates belonging to the suborder Haplorhini. They are known for their agile movements, tree-dwelling habitats, and their ability to grasp objects with their hands.

Monkey Species

There are hundreds of monkey species. Some popular ones include:

  • Squirrel Monkey
  • Chimpanzee
  • Baboon

Monkey Habitat

Monkeys can be found in various habitats including rainforests, savannas, and mangrove forests.

Fun Facts about Monkeys

  • Monkeys have opposable thumbs, allowing them to grip objects and use tools.
  • They are highly social animals and live in groups called troops or bands.
  • Monkeys have excellent agility and are known for their acrobatic abilities.
  • Some monkey species, like the Capuchin monkey, use stones as tools to crack open nuts.
